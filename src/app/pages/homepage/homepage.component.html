Meet the team

Lash

Hi, I’m Lash, the voice behind the mic! Hailing from the vibrant city of Takoradi. I’m a software engineer by day and a creative enthusiast by night. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me sketching out my ideas or capturing moments through my lens as a passionate photographer. I love exploring the intersection of technology, art, and storytelling, and I’m thrilled to bring those passions to life on this podcast. Join me as we dive into fascinating conversations, share insights, and connect with amazing voices from all walks of life. Let’s create something unforgettable together!

Kojo

Hi, I’m Kojo, a UX designer, technologist, and entrepreneur from Agona Swedru with a deep passion for crafting digital experiences that don’t just look good but feel right. I believe great design isn’t just about aesthetics, it’s about creating intuitive, meaningful interactions that solve real-world problems. Beyond pixels and wireframes, I’m always thinking about the bigger picture—how technology can drive businesses forward and create opportunities. Whether it’s building a brand, launching a product, or designing user experiences that truly connect, I love turning ideas into reality. Let’s reimagine the future, one design at a time!
